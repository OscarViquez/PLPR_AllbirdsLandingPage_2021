A Technical Trail Shoe — Made From Nature, For Nature

Our first-ever running shoe designed for the trails is sustainably-engineered and intentionally designed for durability, comfort, and performance..

Easier On The Planet

What do you get when you combine the cushion of SweetFoam®, the comfort of wool, and the breathability of eucalyptus tree fiber? The Trail Runner SWT — or "Sugar, Wool, Tree", featuring a eucalyptus tree fiber and wool blend upper, a SweetFoam® sugarcane midsole, and a natural rubber outsole, made from nature for nature.
